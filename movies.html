The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

Titanic (1997)

Directed by James Cameron, this epic romance film tells the story of Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

The Notebook (2004)

Directed by Nick Cassavetes and based on the novel by Nicholas Sparks, this romantic drama follows the enduring love story of Noah and Allie, who are separated by social differences and World War II.

Pride and Prejudice (2005)

Directed by Joe Wright and based on Jane Austen's classic novel, this adaptation stars Keira Knightley and Matthew Macfadyen and tells the story of Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy as they navigate societal expectations and their own misunderstandings on the path to love.
